Characters

Ken

Ken has no shell, which means he has no special abilities. Keep searching for shells!

Boogly Shell

While Ken has the Boogly Shell he has the slime bullet power-up.

Skoogly Shell

While Ken has the Skoogly Shell he has the power to perform a medium jump.

Skala Shell

When Ken has the Skala Shell you will be able to perform a short jump.

Oogly Shell

When Ken has the Oogly shell he has the ability to become invincible.
